= Mid Squash Test

Start with 9 commits.

[listing]
----
$ add_repo
$ add_commits 1 8
$ git log --format=%s
| 8
| 7
| 6
| 5
| 4
| 3
| 2
| 1
| Initial
----

Squash the 3 in the middle.

[listing]
----
$ git-squash-range HEAD~5 HEAD~3 >/dev/null
$ git log --format=%s
| 8
| 7
| 6
| Squashed
| 2
| 1
| Initial
----
